About Me

I'm Jerome Hardaway, a software engineer, blogger, instructor, CTO, and mentor based in Atlanta. My passion lies in leveraging technology to solve complex problems, mentoring the next generation of developers, and continuously advancing my expertise in AI and coding. My ultimate goal is to become a leading expert in AI and coding, and to help others in thier career.

My Journey

Jerome Hardaway is an accomplished individual who has made significant contributions in the fields of technology, entrepreneurship, and veteran advocacy. Known for his unwavering dedication and passion, Jerome has emerged as a prominent figure, impacting various fronts.

Jerome's journey began with his service in the United States Air Force, where he served for six years as a Security Forces member. During his time in the military, he developed a deep understanding of the challenges faced by veterans transitioning to civilian life, particularly in the tech industry.

Driven by his desire to make a difference, Jerome founded Vets Who Code, a non-profit organization aimed at equipping veterans with the necessary skills to thrive in the tech sector. Through innovative training programs, mentorship, and career guidance, Vets Who Code has empowered numerous veterans, helping them secure meaningful employment and build successful careers in technology.

As an advocate for diversity and inclusion in the tech industry, Jerome has been vocal about the need for equal opportunities for underrepresented groups. He has actively worked to bridge the gap and create a more inclusive environment, speaking at conferences, participating in panel discussions, and collaborating with organizations committed to promoting diversity.

Jerome's expertise and influence have not gone unnoticed. He has been recognized for his outstanding contributions, receiving numerous accolades and honors, including being named to the Social Entrepreneurship category's Forbes 30 Under 30 list. His work has also been featured in prominent media outlets, showcasing the impact he has made and inspiring others to follow in his footsteps.

Beyond his advocacy and entrepreneurship, Jerome is an avid learner and technology enthusiast. He continues to expand his knowledge in emerging fields, always staying ahead of the curve and seeking innovative ways to address societal challenges.

Jerome Hardaway's tireless efforts, visionary leadership, and commitment to empowering others have made him a respected figure in the tech community. His work has transformed countless veterans' lives and paved the way for a more inclusive and diverse industry. With his remarkable accomplishments and ongoing dedication, Jerome serves as an inspiration to aspiring entrepreneurs, advocates, and individuals striving to create positive change in their communities.
